About me

Hello! My name is Riccardo, I am 20 years old, and I am a young aspiring designer based in The Hague, Netherlands.

From a very young age, I found that my actions were curiosity driven. Raised under sunny Italian skies with deep ackowledgement of my Transilvanian roots, I have developed a passion for expression and communication through any means possible. I enjoy exploring and understanding the way people relate to concepts and ideas, and with time I have found that design truly fuels my soul.

As an aspiring UX Designer, I would like to help people make conscious decisions about their passions and goals. I believe a good design does not necessarily mean an easy design: in the future, that has the potential to become a dangerous concept. Instead, I strongly believe that meaningful design is strong design, which can help us become a little bit better in our interactions and connections, day by day.

My studies

I'm currently pursuing a B.Sc. in User Experience Design at De Haagse Hogeschool (The Hague, Netherlands). My expected graduation date will be in 2022. I have studied essential Graphic Design at Libera Accademia di Belle Arti (Rimini, Italy) during 2018-19.
